Our Handcrafting Process

How we create your one-of-a-kind belt buckle

1

Design

We start with your logo or design concept and create a detailed plan for your buckle.

2

Cutting

Each piece is cut by hand with a jeweler's saw from solid sheets of premium metal.

3

Crafting

Multiple steps of polishing, texturing, soldering, and adding colors in controlled conditions.

4

Finishing

Final detailing and signature numbering to create your unique, lasting piece.
