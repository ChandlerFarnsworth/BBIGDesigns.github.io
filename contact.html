Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

The production time for a custom belt buckle typically ranges from 3-4 weeks from approval of the design. This timeline allows for our meticulous handcrafting process. Rush orders may be available in certain circumstances - please contact us to discuss your specific timeline needs.

No, there is no minimum order quantity. Since each of our buckles is 100% cut by hand, we can create a single custom buckle or hundreds for your event or organization. This is one of the advantages of our handcrafted approach versus mass production methods.

To provide an accurate quote, we need to know the following:

  • The design/logo you want to use (a digital file is ideal)
  • The metals and colors you're interested in
  • The quantity needed
  • Your timeline for delivery

The more detail you can provide, the more accurate our quote will be. Don't worry if you don't have all these details yet - we can help guide you through the process.

Yes, all of our buckles come with a lifetime guarantee. We stand behind the quality of our craftsmanship and materials. If there is ever a problem with your buckle that is related to craftsmanship or materials, we will repair or replace it at no charge.

Yes, we can match specific pantone colors in our enamel work. Our artists are skilled at color matching and can replicate your brand colors with amazing accuracy. We can also replicate detailed design elements, logos, and graphics. Just provide us with your design specifications, and we'll work to create an exact representation in buckle form.

Yes, we ship worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Please contact us for specific shipping information for your location. We work with reliable shipping partners to ensure your buckles arrive safely, no matter where you are.
